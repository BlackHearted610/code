  1. Pre-classic

    This was the first (private) version of minecraft! This was the time that minecraft was called "Cave Game", nothing much happened in this update besides well, minecraft!

  2. Classic

    Play minecraft classic here!

  3. indev

    Survival mode was officialy added, a game mode where you spawn into a world with nothing and you have to grind and explore to survive. As the name states.
    As you can see this mode diden't look different but there was a new game mode!

  4. Infdev

    During mineraft infdev the world borders were set to infinet, rather before the world borders were 256x256.

  5. Alpha

    This was one year before the official release!

    Minecraft alpha took away survival mode, but added multiplayer. Allowing you to play with your friends and starting a new thing called servers. On servers you could play new games like spleef, and boxing! Also Notch gave everyone 999 items from the start.

  6. Beta

    This is it. The last paragaph. Minercraft beta. This was the last release before the offical release. During minecraft beta editon there was a 50% price increase, and added new friendly mobs including the sheep, pigs, and squids.

    There were also new tree types, and beds were added to. also new blocks like slabs, locked chests, cobwebs, new saplings, new rails, tall grass, trapdoors, and pistons. There were also new items and redstone, like sheers, so you didden't have to kill sheep to harvest their wool, and new weather.
